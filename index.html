WHO ARE WE...?
We are UEM Aeromodelling Club, a student-run organization under University of Engineering and Management, Jaipur, we are dedicated to inspiring aviation enthusiasts, through innovative projects and training programs, it nurtures a passion for aviation among students.

OUR MOTTO...
Our members construct model planes from scratch with materials like paper and balsa wood, engaging in both the hobby and sport of model aircraft. The club's motto, "Lifting future beyond vision," reflects its commitment to advancing aviation through research and development.

ORIGINALITY AND INVENTIVENESS...
We lead the way in aeromodelling innovation. Our members design, build, and fly state-of-the-art model aircraft, exploring the latest in aerodynamics and flight technology. Join us to turn visionary ideas into airborne realities.
