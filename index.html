WHO ARE WE...?
We are UEM Aeromodelling Club, a student-run organization under University of Engineering and Management, Jaipur, we are dedicated to inspiring aviation enthusiasts, through innovative projects and training programs, it nurtures a passion for aviation among students.
Welcome to
UEM AEROMODELLING CLUB
OUR MOTTO...
The club's motto, "Lifting future beyond vision," reflects its commitment to advancing aviation through research and development. Our members construct model planes from scratch with materials like paper and balsa wood, engaging in both the hobby and sport of model aircraft.
Welcome to
UEM AEROMODELLING CLUB
ORIGINALITY AND INVENTIVENESS...
We lead the way in aeromodelling and innovation. Our members design, build, and fly state-of-the-art model aircraft, exploring the latest in aerodynamics and flight technology. Join us to turn visionary ideas into airborne realities.
